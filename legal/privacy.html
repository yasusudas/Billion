Privacy

Privacy and data handling

This public site is a static storefront for self-serve digital kits. It is designed to let buyers read product information without using a contact form, account sign-up, or file upload on this website.

Public site posture

What this website does not ask for

No forms

The public storefront does not include contact forms, lead forms, file upload forms, or support message forms.

No client-side scripts

The public HTML pages do not use client-side JavaScript, analytics scripts, tracking pixels, or custom checkout code.

No private documents

The public pages do not include upload or input fields for customer logs, recordings, credentials, private prompts, contracts, or identity documents.

No payment-detail handling

Hosted checkout, when enabled, must be handled by the payment provider. These static pages do not contain payment-detail input fields.

Payment provider

Checkout data is separate

When hosted checkout is activated, checkout and receipt data are handled by the payment provider under its own systems and account-owner settings. Do not put payout, identity, bank, tax, payment identifier, card, or private buyer data in this static repository.

Operational boundary

Use public examples when evaluating the kits

The templates are intended for internal business planning. When using them, avoid placing personal data, sensitive customer details, credentials, private logs, or regulated decision records into public examples or support materials.
