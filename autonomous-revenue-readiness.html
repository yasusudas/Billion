Revenue can start only through hosted checkout

Billion AI Operations Studio is prepared as a no-contact digital kit storefront. Activation boundary

The remaining blocker is financial account activation

Hosted checkout URLs are not connected yet. Until the payment account owner creates provider-hosted checkout links, completes required account settings, and supplies non-sensitive live checkout URLs, this website cannot collect payment or confirm an order.

After those URLs exist, the static activation guard validates product IDs, exact JPY amounts, HTTPS provider checkout hosts, success pages, and absence of private values before public purchase buttons can be enabled.

Autonomous flow after activation

How a buyer can pay without manual contact

  1. Find: Buyer lands on a public product, resource, comparison, or self-check page.
  2. Decide: Buyer reviews kit scope, non-fit cases, samples, terms, and commercial disclosure.
  3. Pay: Buyer uses the matching provider-hosted checkout link after activation.
  4. Receive: Successful checkout routes to the matching delivery manifest or provider-gated delivery route.
  5. Resolve: Any post-purchase review is limited to receipt-backed duplicate purchase, failed delivery, or clear file defect cases.
