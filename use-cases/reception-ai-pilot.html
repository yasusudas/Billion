Use case

Reception AI pilot planning before live traffic

For teams considering AI-assisted reception, missed-call triage, or inquiry routing, the safest first step is to define allowed actions, fallback rules, and acceptance checks before production use.

Fit

When this use case applies

Inquiry volume is uneven

The team receives repeated questions, missed calls, or routing work that could be summarized or triaged.

Final authority must stay human

Bookings, refunds, safety, medical, legal, and payment decisions must remain outside AI authority.

Pilot criteria are unclear

The team needs acceptance checks for tone, escalation, handoff, prohibited responses, and stop conditions.

Recommended kit

Start with Reception AI Pilot Kit

The Reception AI Pilot Kit provides inquiry mapping, fallback rules, prohibited response checks, and acceptance criteria for front-office pilot planning. It is not a managed reception service.
