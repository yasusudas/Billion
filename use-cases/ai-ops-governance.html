Use case

AI operations governance before rollout

For teams that are moving from ad hoc AI experiments to repeated internal workflows, the first problem is usually not model choice. It is ownership, approval, evidence, and fallback discipline.

Fit

When this use case applies

AI output affects operations

The workflow drafts, summarizes, routes, or recommends actions that staff use in routine work.

Review rules are informal

People know who usually checks the output, but the approval point and stop condition are not written down.

Expansion is planned

The team wants to increase usage but needs a clearer baseline before wider access or custom implementation.

Recommended kit

Start with AI Ops Diagnostic Kit

The AI Ops Diagnostic Kit provides templates for mapping workflow owners, data classes, tool access, approval points, fallback paths, and stop conditions. It is a planning kit, not a legal, security, or compliance audit.
