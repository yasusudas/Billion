Store Support Delivery Terms Disclosure

Receipt support

Support boundary after hosted checkout

Billion AI Operations Studio is a no-contact self-serve storefront. After hosted checkout is activated, support-adjacent review is limited to duplicate purchase, failed delivery, or clear file defect issues tied to the payment provider receipt or purchase history. Pre-sale consultation, custom setup, and manual onboarding are not provided.

Covered cases

What can be reviewed after purchase

Duplicate purchase

A repeated purchase of the same digital kit may be reviewed through the provider receipt route after checkout activation.

Failed delivery

A successful hosted checkout that does not reach the expected delivery manifest or delivery service may be reviewed.

Clear file defect

A missing, corrupted, or materially incorrect file in the purchased kit may be reviewed after fulfillment is active.

Provider records

Only real provider receipt or purchase-history evidence can confirm an order, receipt, refund review, or payment fact.

Not covered

No manual advisory route

Receipt support does not include pre-sale questions, product selection advice, custom consulting, implementation review, data analysis, live setup, workshops, buyer-specific recommendations, or manual training. Buyers should use the public product pages, comparison page, samples, FAQ, terms, and no-contact operation page before checkout.

Current status

Checkout is not active yet

Hosted checkout links are not connected yet. Until provider-hosted checkout is active, there is no purchase receipt, no delivery claim, no refund review, and no payment fact for this storefront.

If a payment provider requires a direct customer-service email, phone number, address, contact form, or similar manual channel before activation, that requirement must be resolved by the payment account owner because this no-contact storefront does not add manual buyer communication routes.
