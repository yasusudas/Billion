FAQ

Common questions before buying

Short answers about what the self-serve kits include, how delivery works, what is not included, and why payments remain disabled until hosted checkout is activated.

What does Billion AI Operations Studio sell?

It sells self-serve digital business kits for AI operations diagnostics, public RFP monitoring workflow setup, and reception AI pilot planning. Each kit contains editable templates, checklists, and operating guidance.

Is a sales call required?

No. The self-serve kits are designed for purchase and delivery without a sales call, manual onboarding, direct message, or contact form. Custom services are separate and require a written quotation before work begins.

Are payments currently active?

No. The storefront, product pages, delivery pages, and terms are prepared, but hosted checkout links are not yet connected. Until checkout is activated, this site does not collect payment and does not represent that any order, invoice, contract, or payment has occurred.

How will delivery work after checkout is active?

Each hosted checkout link should redirect to the matching delivery page or a dedicated delivery service after successful payment. Delivery pages are prepared as noindex manifests, but full paid files should not be published in this public verification repository while checkout is pending.

Can I preview the product before checkout?

Yes. The public sample page shows representative rows and structures without linking to the full delivery files.

What is not included?

The kits do not include managed implementation, legal advice, procurement compliance advice, security certification, medical diagnosis, financial advice, customer-support outsourcing, or a guarantee of business results.

Can I decide without contacting anyone?

Yes. The purchase self-check, comparison, samples, resource pages, and kit detail pages are designed to support no-contact purchase decisions.

What about refunds?

Self-serve digital kits are fulfilled immediately after successful hosted checkout. Refunds may be considered for duplicate purchases, failed delivery, or clear file defects reported through the payment receipt support route after checkout is active.

Does this site ask for personal data?

The public site has no forms, no client-side scripts, and no tracking code. Hosted checkout, when enabled, is handled by the payment processor.
