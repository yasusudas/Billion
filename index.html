How work is delivered

Engagement process

  1. Discovery: We confirm the business objective, workflow, available documents, and constraints.
  2. Quotation: We provide a written scope, price, schedule, and acceptance criteria.
  3. Delivery: Work is performed remotely through review sessions, analysis, and written deliverables.
  4. Acceptance: The client reviews the deliverables against the agreed scope and requests corrections for in-scope defects if needed.
