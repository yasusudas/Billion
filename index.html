Business profile

What this business sells

Billion AI Operations Studio sells professional B2B services for AI operations governance, business process discovery, RFP and proposal monitoring setup, and early-stage AI pilot planning.

Business category: Professional services / Consulting. Services are advisory and operational in nature, delivered remotely through workshops, operational review, document analysis, and written deliverables.

Services are delivered remotely through workshops, document review, operational analysis, and written deliverables. We do not sell consumer financial products, investment advice, medical advice, regulated legal advice, gambling products, or physical goods.

Services

Core offerings

Primary service

AI Operations Diagnostic

A paid diagnostic for teams using AI agents, LLM workflows, or automation in internal operations. The output includes an operating-risk summary, control matrix, incident-prevention checklist, and 90-day action plan.

Setup service

RFP and Proposal Radar Setup

A workflow setup service that helps teams monitor public procurement, proposal opportunities, and account-specific buying signals using clear, auditable operating steps.

Pilot planning

Reception AI Pilot Blueprint

A discovery and planning service for reception, inquiry handling, and front-office AI pilots. The output defines scope, risks, fallback paths, and pilot acceptance criteria.

How work is delivered

Engagement process

  1. Discovery: We confirm the business objective, workflow, available documents, and constraints.
  2. Quotation: We provide a written scope, price, schedule, and acceptance criteria.
  3. Delivery: Work is performed remotely through review sessions, analysis, and written deliverables.
  4. Acceptance: The client reviews the deliverables against the agreed scope and requests corrections for in-scope defects if needed.

Policies

Refund, cancellation, and restrictions

Refund policy

If a paid engagement is cancelled before the kickoff session and before work begins, the unused service fee is refundable. After work begins, refunds are limited to undelivered scope, unless otherwise agreed in the written quotation.

Cancellation policy

Clients may request cancellation by written notice. Completed work, scheduled workshops already delivered, and accepted deliverables are not refundable unless a separate written agreement says otherwise.

Service restrictions

We do not provide regulated financial advice, legal representation, medical diagnosis, consumer lending, gambling, physical goods, or employment decisions. Services are advisory and operational in nature.

Privacy

Client documents are used only for the agreed engagement. We avoid storing sensitive personal data unless it is necessary for the service and explicitly approved by the client.
