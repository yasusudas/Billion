Business profile

What this business sells

Billion AI Operations Studio sells self-serve digital business kits and professional B2B services for AI operations governance, business process discovery, RFP and proposal monitoring setup, and early-stage AI pilot planning.

Business category: Professional services / Consulting. Self-serve products are downloadable templates and checklists. Professional services are advisory and operational in nature, delivered remotely through workshops, operational review, document analysis, and written deliverables.

Policies

Refund, cancellation, and restrictions

Refund policy

Self-serve digital kits are downloadable products. Refunds may be considered for duplicate purchases, failed delivery, or clear file defects reported through the payment receipt support route. Custom engagements follow the written quotation.

Cancellation policy

Self-serve kit purchases are fulfilled immediately after checkout and cannot be cancelled after successful delivery except where required by law or separately agreed. Custom services may be cancelled under the written quotation.

Service restrictions

We do not provide regulated financial advice, legal representation, medical diagnosis, consumer lending, gambling, physical goods, or employment decisions.

Privacy

The public site has no forms and does not request personal documents, customer logs, recordings, payment details, or passwords. Hosted checkout, when enabled, is handled by the payment processor.

Support model

No manual sales contact required

Self-serve products are designed to be purchased and delivered without a sales meeting, outreach message, or manual onboarding. Custom services remain available only after a written quotation is prepared.
