## Decision Rules

- Move to `Review now` only when the source is public, the deadline is visible, and the fit reason can be checked by a human.
- Move to `Prepare brief` only when the scorecard owner can explain the opportunity in one paragraph.
- Move to `Monitor` when the signal is useful but not actionable this week.
- Move to `Reject` when source access, eligibility, scope, or deadline risk makes review impractical.
