# Reception AI Pilot Kit

Billion AI Operations Studio

## Purpose

Use this kit to scope a reception AI pilot around missed calls, inquiry routing, fallback paths, prohibited responses, and acceptance criteria. It is not for medical, safety, legal, hiring, or final booking decisions.

## Files

- `reception-inquiry-map.csv`
- `reception-fallback-rules.csv`
- `reception-acceptance-checklist.csv`

## How to Use

1. List common inquiry types and their allowed responses.
2. Define where AI can draft, summarize, or route messages.
3. Write human fallback rules before any customer-facing pilot.
4. Use the acceptance checklist before enabling real customer traffic.

## Acceptance Criteria

- The AI cannot confirm final reservations, payments, medical guidance, safety advice, or legal claims.
- Human fallback conditions are visible to operators.
- Pilot success is measured by routing quality and reduced missed follow-up, not by replacing accountability.
