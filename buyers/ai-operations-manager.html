When this role should care

AI use is spreading informally

Different teams use LLMs or agents, but workflow ownership and review timing are not written.

Approvals are unclear

Outputs may affect customers, proposals, support, or internal decisions before a named reviewer sees them.

Fallbacks are not rehearsed

Operators do not have a documented manual path when the AI workflow should pause.
