Delivery and access

How self-serve delivery works

This page explains the intended delivery flow for digital kits after hosted checkout is activated. Payments are not yet enabled, so no order or delivery claim is made here.

Flow

Planned checkout-to-delivery sequence

  1. Buyer reviews public pages: Product details, FAQ, samples, comparison, and policies can be read without a form.
  2. Hosted checkout opens: A payment-provider hosted checkout link is used after account-owner activation.
  3. Successful checkout redirects: The provider redirects the buyer to the matching kit delivery page.
  4. Buyer downloads files: The buyer uses the delivered templates internally under the stated boundaries.

Limits

Access model and limitations

Delivery pages and product files are prepared for hosted-checkout success redirects and are marked noindex through metadata and response headers. Static success URLs are not strong authentication. If stronger access control is required, use a dedicated delivery service after hosted checkout is active.

The public storefront does not include input fields for payment details, passwords, customer logs, private documents, or support messages. Hosted checkout, when enabled, is handled by the payment provider.

Current status

Checkout is not active yet

The storefront and delivery structure are ready, but hosted checkout links are not yet connected. Until the payment account owner completes setup, this site cannot collect payment and does not confirm any order, invoice, contract, or payment.
