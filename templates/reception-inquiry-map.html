Use

What the inquiry map clarifies

Allowed action

The team defines whether AI may share public information, collect preferences, draft a reply, or route only.

Human fallback

Each inquiry type includes the condition that moves the flow back to a human operator.

Prohibited action

Planning rows make final reservations, payment decisions, safety guidance, and legal claims explicit exclusions.
