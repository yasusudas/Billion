When a tracker helps

Terminology shifts

The same opportunity may appear as AI operations, LLM governance, DX support, or inquiry routing.

Review time is limited

Priority fields help choose which keywords deserve weekly review first.

Fit reasoning is inconsistent

A reason-to-monitor column keeps keyword choices tied to business fit instead of broad search volume.
