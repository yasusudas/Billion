No-contact operation

Self-serve storefront without manual sales contact

Billion AI Operations Studio is operated as a passive public storefront for digital business kits. Buyers evaluate public pages, use provider-hosted checkout after activation, and receive digital delivery without a sales call, direct message, contact form, custom quote conversation, or manual onboarding.

Allowed surfaces

Where the service can be discovered

Public website

Product pages, comparison pages, samples, FAQ, legal pages, and resources are publicly readable without a password.

Search discovery

The sitemap and public resource pages support organic discovery by search engines and AI crawlers.

Hosted checkout

Payment buttons may link only to provider-hosted checkout pages after account-owner activation and validation.

Digital delivery

Successful checkout may redirect to the matching delivery manifest or a dedicated delivery service.

Not used

Manual contact routes are excluded

The operating model does not use cold email, direct messages, manual contact forms, manual social posting, scraped prospect lists, discovery calls, manual follow-up, customer-managed support inboxes, live setup, or individually negotiated invoices.

This website intentionally contains no forms, upload fields, client-side scripts, tracking pixels, custom card-entry fields, phone number, or public pre-sale mailbox.
