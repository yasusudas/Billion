RFP Radar Workflow

A lightweight operating pattern for reviewing public procurement and proposal opportunities without relying on memory, private buyer data, or manual one-off searches.

View RFP Radar Setup Kit Compare all kits

Workflow

Weekly review sequence

  1. Source map: List official public portals, agency pages, newsletters, and review frequency.
  2. Keyword set: Track service terms, buyer terms, technology terms, geographic terms, and exclusion terms.
  3. Fit score: Score each opportunity by scope fit, eligibility, deadline, evidence required, and delivery capacity.
  4. Decision: Mark each item as watch, qualify, prepare, skip, or blocked with a short reason.
  5. Review note: Keep only public-source notes and avoid storing private buyer details in the workflow.

Operating rules

Keep the review repeatable

Separate source and opinion

Record where the opportunity came from before adding fit notes or next actions.

Use exclusion terms

Filter out notices that are outside scope, outside geography, or require unavailable certifications.

Review at a fixed cadence

A short weekly review is easier to maintain than a long search session when a deadline appears.
