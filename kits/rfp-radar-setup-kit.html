First 30 minutes

How to use it after purchase

  1. Add five official public sources or permitted newsletters to the source map.
  2. Write ten keyword variants that reflect buyer problems, service categories, and geography.
  3. Review one source manually and log every relevant notice in the weekly review sheet.
  4. Move the strongest signals into the opportunity scorecard and response readiness scorecard.
  5. Summarize the week in the brief template before deciding whether proposal work is worth starting.
