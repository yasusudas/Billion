First 60 minutes

How to use it after purchase

  1. Open the team rollout board and list up to six departments or workflows.
  2. Choose the first workflow that already has an owner and a clear review need.
  3. Use the AI Ops control matrix for ownership, approval, fallback, and evidence notes.
  4. Add the workflow to the department adoption calendar with review question and stop condition.
  5. Prepare one manager briefing note before expanding to other departments.
