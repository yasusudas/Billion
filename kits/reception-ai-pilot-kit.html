Purchase fit

Buy this if

You are planning a reception or inquiry-handling AI pilot and need to define routing, allowed actions, and human fallback before launch.

Do not buy this if

You need production bot implementation, live customer handling, final booking decisions, payment decisions, or regulated advice.

Best buyer role

Front-office managers, operations leads, and pilot owners who can define internal routing and escalation rules.

First 30 minutes

How to use it after purchase

  1. List the ten most common inquiry types in the inquiry map.
  2. Mark each inquiry as answer, route, collect basic information, or human fallback.
  3. Write prohibited actions for pricing, diagnosis, complaint handling, payment, and final confirmation cases.
  4. Run the dry-run script on high-risk examples and record results in the operator review log.
  5. Use the acceptance checklist to decide which test cases must pass before pilot traffic expands.
