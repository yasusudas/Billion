First 30 minutes

How to use it after purchase

  1. Open the control matrix and list three AI-assisted workflows that are already active or planned.
  2. Fill owner, data class, approval point, and fallback columns for the highest-risk workflow first.
  3. Run the risk checklist against that workflow and mark unknowns as follow-up tasks.
  4. Move the first follow-up tasks into the 10-business-day plan with owners and review dates.
