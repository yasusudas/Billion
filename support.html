After purchase

Receipt-based support only after checkout activation

After hosted checkout is activated, purchaser support is limited to the payment-provider receipt route configured by the payment account owner. This support boundary is intended for duplicate purchases, failed delivery, or clear file defects. It does not include custom advisory work, implementation review, data analysis, live setup, or manual training.
