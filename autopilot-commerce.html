No-touch commerce boundaries

Billion AI Operations Studio is configured for fixed-price, self-serve digital kit sales. The approved revenue path is public discovery, buyer self-selection, provider-hosted checkout after activation, and digital delivery. Manual outreach, manual posting, contact forms, sales calls, custom quotes, and buyer-specific onboarding are excluded.
